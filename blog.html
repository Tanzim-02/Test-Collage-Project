Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2023

Our university is excited to offer new certificate and online programs for 2023. These programs provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their chosen fields and are taught by experienced faculty.


Our certificate programs offer a focused and flexible way to enhance your career or explore a new area of interest. They include options such as:

  • Digital Marketing
  • Data Science
  • Project Management
  • Graphic Design
  • Human Resource Management


Our online programs offer the convenience and flexibility of studying from anywhere, at your own pace. They are a great option for busy professionals or those who may not be able to commit to a traditional on-campus program. Some examples of online programs we are offering in 2023 include:

  • Master of Machine Learning
  • Master of Science in Data Science
  • Master of Education (MEd)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Science in Nursing (MSN)


Whether you are looking to advance your career, pursue further education, or simply enrich your knowledge, our certificate and online programs offer something for everyone. If you have any questions or would like more information, please don't hesitate to contact us.
